# Adidas-Sales-Analyzing-Data_Power-BI

**Project Overview**

The goal of this project is to study and understand the sales performance of Adidas products. Using Power BI, this project shows how sales change across different regions, products, and time periods. It helps to find which areas or products are doing well and where improvements are needed.


**Dataset Description**

1.	Retailer – Name of the store or outlet selling Adidas products.
2.	Retailer ID – Unique identifier for each retailer.
3.	Invoice Date – Date of the sales transaction.
4.	Region / State / City – Location information for regional sales analysis.
5.	Product – Type of Adidas item sold (e.g., footwear, apparel, accessories).
6.	Unit Price & Unit Cost – Selling and cost price per item.
7.	Total Sales – Total revenue generated.
8.	Operating Profit / Margin – Profitability of the transaction.
9.	Sales Method – Type of channel used (Online / In-Store).
    

**Business Objectives**

•	Analyze overall Adidas sales performance using key KPIs — sales, profit, units sold, and margin 
•	Identify top-performing regions, products, and retailers
•	Identify top-performing regions, products, and retailers.
•	Find areas with low performance to improve business strategie.


**Visuals**

•	KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Profit, Units Sold, and Average Price per unit, Average Margine
•	Total Sales by Month (Area Chart) : shows monthly sales trends from January to December, highlighting peak sales months.
•	Sales by Sales Method (Pie Chart) : compares In-store, Online, and Outlet sales performance.
•	Sales by Product (Bar Chart) : shows which Adidas product categories (e.g., footwear, apparel) perform best
•	Sales by Region (Dount Chart) : displays regional contribution to total sales (West, Northeast, South, Southeast, Midwest).
•	Sales by State (Map Chart)  : visualizes total sales distribution across U.S. states.


**Tools & Technologies**

•	Microsoft Power BI for data visualization and report building
•	Power Query for data transformation and cleaning
•	DAX Functions: SUM, COUNT, AVERAGE, IF
•	AI Visuals: Key Influencers, Decomposition Tree
•	Slicers for interactivity and dynamic filtering


**Slicer Interaction**  

Added interactive Slicers for Region and Invoice Date
•	Region → Filters visuals by Region (West, Midwest, Northeast, South, Southeast)
•	Invoice Date → Filters visuals by sales time period
All visuals update automatically when slicers are applied, allowing users to analyze sales, profit, and product performance interactively.
